MaskFeat Training Analysis

Interactive Visualization of Model Training Performance

Overview

This dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of MaskFeat model training across different configurations and architectures. Compare the performance of different warmup epochs and batch sizes, track validation accuracy across ViT-B and ViT-L models, and explore projected outcomes through interactive charts.

Key Findings: The optimized ViT-B configuration (5 warmup epochs, batch size 512) demonstrates superior generalization with a mean gap of 1.24%. ViT-L achieves 18.44% validation error at epoch 50, showing excellent convergence and approaching the 14.3% target, demonstrating the power of larger models with efficient training.
